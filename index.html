Our Values

Zahra's Dream Occasions was founded in 2023 by Zahra. After years of planning events for friends and family, Zahra decided to turn her passion into a business. She began by planning small events, such as birthdays and anniversaries, and quickly expanded to larger events, such as weddings and corporate events. Zahra's Dream Occasions has since grown into a full-service event planning company, with a team of dedicated professionals who are committed to bringing your vision to life.
